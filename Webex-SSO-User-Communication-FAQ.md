# Webex SSO Transition: User FAQ & Communication Plan
**Audience:** 2000 Enterprise Users  
**Objective:** De escalate user anxiety and reduce Help Desk volume during the Entra ID SAML migration.

## Implementation Strategy
The migration was designed to be "invisible." However, since SSO enforcement eventually triggers a re-authentication event, this FAQ was distributed to empower users to manage their own transition.

## Frequently Asked Questions - What you need to know

### Q1: Will I need to do anything?
**No.** The process will feel identical to signing into your corporate email. Since your Authenticator is already a requirement for our environment, you are already set up.

### Q2: Do I need to download or install anything new?
**No.** Whatever version of Webex you already have on your phone or computer will work fine. No updates or installs are required.

### Q2: Will I lose my messages, meetings, or contacts?
**No.** SSO only changes the "door" you walk through to get into the app. Everything inside your Webex account remains exactly as it was.

### Q3: What happens to my Webex login?
**Signin out and back in again** At some point after the switchover, the Webex App will log you out. Simply sign back in using your standard company credentials and your Authenticator App. This may happen hours or even weeks after the switchover.

### Q4: What about the Webex App on my phone?
**Same answer as above.** At some point your Webex mobile app will prompt you to log back in. Just sign in the same way you would on your laptop or desktop. Your standard company credential and Authenticator App.

### Q5: Can I control when the logout happens?
**Yes.** If you prefer not to be logged out during a meeting, you can manually sign out and sign back in on your own schedule to "refresh" your session under the new SSO protocol.

On your computer: click your profile picture in the top corner of the Webex app, then select Sign Out

On your phone: tap your profile picture and then tap Sign Out

When you sign back in, you'll go through the new SSO process using your standard company credentials and Authenticator App and you'll be all set with a fresh session on your own terms.

### Q6: What if I get an error?
**Don't Panic.** Your account is safe. Simply contact the Technology Support Line and we will prioritize your reconnection.

### Q7: What if I forget my password?
**Don't Panic** Same process as forgetting your email password. Contact the Technology Support Line or use the existing self service password reset process. Nothing new here.

### Q8: I'm not at work on the day it switches over. Will I be affected?
**Not immediately.** As noted in Q3, the timing of when you'll be prompted to log back in varies by user and device. When you do get prompted, just log in normally using your standard company credentials and Authenticator App.

---
## 💡 Analyst Note
By providing the "Manual Sign Out" option, we successfully shifted the transition from a "forced event" to a "user controlled event," significantly reducing peak hour support requests.
